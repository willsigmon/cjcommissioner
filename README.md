# CJ Turrentine for Vance County Commissioner

Campaign website for Charles "CJ" Turrentine Jr., candidate for Vance County Commissioner District 3 (Sandy Creek District).

## About CJ

**A Public Servant, Not a Politician**

CJ Turrentine is a combat veteran who served in Iraq during Operation Iraqi Freedom. Since returning home, he has dedicated himself to serving Henderson and Vance County through:

- **Chestnut Street Park Beautification** - Raised $140,000 to transform a neglected basketball court into a vibrant community space
- **Pathways 2 Peace Coalition** - Founded a 501(c)(3) to address gun violence in Vance County
- **Hope House** - Developed transitional housing for men leaving homelessness
- **5 Years** as Director of Community Partners of Hope Men's Shelter

## Three Promises

1. **Respect** - Treating everyone with dignity, even those I disagree with
2. **Honesty** - Speaking truth and leading with integrity
3. **Real Effort** - Showing up consistently and putting people first
